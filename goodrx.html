GoodRx

Overview: GoodRx is a healthcare technology company that provides a platform for consumers to compare prescription drug prices and find the best deals on medications. The company aims to make healthcare more affordable and accessible for everyone.

Mission: GoodRx's mission is to help Americans save money on prescription medications by providing transparent pricing and access to discounts across thousands of pharmacies nationwide.

Key Products: The GoodRx platform includes a website and mobile app that allow users to search for medications, compare prices at different pharmacies, and access coupons for discounts. It also provides telehealth services for certain conditions.

Technology: GoodRx leverages technology to aggregate and display real-time pricing data for medications from various pharmacies. The platform uses algorithms to match users with the best pricing options based on their location and specific needs.
